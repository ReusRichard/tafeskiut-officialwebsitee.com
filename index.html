About TAFES KIUT

Historical Background

The Tanzania Fellowship of Evangelical Students (TAFES) is part of a global student movement aimed to spread the gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ through universities and colleges in Tanzania.

TAFES is currently operating in nine regions in Tanzania, spreading the word of the Lord Jesus Christ through colleges and universities with Kampala International University in Tanzania (KIUT) being one of them.

Establishment

TAFES KIUT was established in December 2011 and has grown from 16 members to over 180 student members by 2025.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a national movement reaching every student in all Universities and Colleges in Tanzania with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To Equip University and College students with the Scripture in Evangelism, Discipleship and Leadership Development thereby transforming the Campus, Church and the Society.

Core Values

  • Evangelical

    The Bible is our standard & final authority in all matters of faith & conduct.

  • Inter-denominational

    We provide a common forum for worship across various denominations.

  • Student-centred

    We operate primarily in tertiary institutions and are committed to students' initiative.

  • Unity of Believers

    We provide a forum where believers from different denominations interact.

  • Excellence and Integrity

    We aim to ensure that student members perform academically and are of Godly character.

Core Commitments

  • Leadership Development

    Developing student members to become leaders tomorrow.

  • Discipleship

    Deepening and strengthening spiritual life of students.

  • Evangelism

    Students reaching students with the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ.

  • Missions

    Taking the gospel beyond campuses to other areas and nations.

  • Prayers

    Committed to personal and intercessory prayer meetings.

  • Scripture Engagement

    In-depth study of the Scripture with sound interpretation.

  • Service to Church & Community

    Serving local churches and communities through social work.
